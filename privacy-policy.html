Privacy Policy for Bookmarks to Tab Groups

Last updated: [2024-12-28]

1. Data Collection

Bookmarks to Tab Groups is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what data we collect and how we use it:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not store any of your bookmarks data
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers

2. Data Usage

The extension requires access to:

  • Bookmarks: To read your Chrome bookmarks structure locally for conversion to tab groups rules
  • Downloads: To save the generated JSON file to your local device

3. Local Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your device:

  • Bookmark data is only read and processed within your browser
  • Generated rules are saved directly to your device
  • No data leaves your computer

4. Third-Party Services

This extension:

  • Does not use any third-party services
  • Does not integrate with external APIs
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools

5. Data Retention

Since we don't collect or store any data, there is no data retention policy needed. All generated files are stored locally on your device and managed by you.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the policy and the version number of the extension.
